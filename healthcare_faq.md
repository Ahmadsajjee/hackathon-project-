# Comprehensive Healthcare FAQ

## Telehealth Services
- **Primary-Care Telehealth Visits:** Covered with a $20 copay.
- **Specialist Telehealth Visits:** Require prior authorization.
- **Mental Health Counseling:** Up to 8 virtual counseling sessions per year are covered at no cost.
- **Chronic Disease Monitoring:** Diabetes, hypertension, asthma follow-ups are covered under telehealth.
- **Urgent Care via Telehealth:** Non-emergency conditions like sinus infections, allergies, and minor injuries are covered.

## Mental Health & Wellness
- Virtual therapy sessions are included in your plan.
- Substance abuse treatment programs are available via telehealth.
- Wellness coaching for stress management and nutrition is covered.

## Preventive Care
- Annual physical exams are covered at no cost.
- Vaccinations and screenings (cholesterol, diabetes) are included.
- Smoking cessation programs and weight management counseling are covered.

## Emergency Coverage
- ER visits are covered worldwide.
- Notify the plan within 48 hours for international emergencies.
- Ambulance services are covered if medically necessary.

## Prescription Support
- Renewals and adjustments can be done via telehealth.
- Home delivery of medications is available for chronic conditions.

## Claims & Billing
- Submit claims via the mobile app → Claims → New Claim.
- Processing takes 5–7 business days.
- Check claim status online or via customer support.

## Provider Network
- Use the Provider Finder at https://example-insurer.com/providers.
- Filter by specialty, location, and telehealth availability.

## Insurance Terms Explained
- **Copay:** Fixed amount you pay for a covered service.
- **Deductible:** Amount you pay before insurance starts covering costs.
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum:** The most you pay in a year for covered services.

## Accessibility & Patient Support
- 24/7 nurse hotline for urgent questions.
- Language interpretation services available for telehealth.
- Mobile app supports screen readers and voice commands.

## HIPAA & Privacy
- All telehealth sessions are encrypted and HIPAA-compliant.
- Your data is never shared without consent.
